The Product Vision: An Intelligent Hub

Project Sentry is designed as a comprehensive, data-centric platform focused on solving core coordination and quality issues, from initial model review to advanced, AI-driven analysis.

📦

Model Federation & Viewing

Aggregate IFC models. View, rotate, create sections, and inspect object properties.

💥

Clash & Duplicate Detection

Identify geometric clashes and redundant elements to ensure model accuracy.

📋

Quantity Takeoff

Automate the extraction of key quantities like area and volume from models.

📈

Model Health Dashboard

Key Differentiator: Proactively monitor model quality and performance KPIs.

🔍

Advanced Analysis

Perform rule-based validation and check for data completeness and standards compliance.

💬

Conversational AI

Future-Ready: Use natural language prompts to perform complex queries and tasks.
